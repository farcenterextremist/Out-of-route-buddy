# Meta-Research Checklist — Improve Research Quality

**Purpose:** Meta-Researchers use this checklist to improve the quality of research produced by primary researchers. Answer before or alongside Phase 0.1 research.

**References:** [IMPROVEMENT_LOOP_TEAMS.md](./IMPROVEMENT_LOOP_TEAMS.md), [IMPROVEMENT_LOOP_ROUTINE.md](./IMPROVEMENT_LOOP_ROUTINE.md)

---

## Five Questions

| # | Question | Action |
|---|----------|--------|
| 1 | **What did last research miss?** | Review last summary; identify gaps. |
| 2 | **Better sources?** | Could we use different docs, web sources, or tools? |
| 3 | **Clearer output?** | Is the one-line research note actionable? |
| 4 | **Gaps?** | What blind spots exist? What should we have asked? |
| 5 | **One improvement for this run** | Propose one concrete change to research method or output. |

---

## Output

One-line meta-note for summary: "Research quality: X. Gaps: Y. Suggested improvement: Z."

---

## When to Run

- **Phase 0.0b** — Optional; when running, answer checklist and add one-line to summary.
- **IMPROVEMENT_LOOP_TEAMS** — Meta-Researchers use this checklist.
