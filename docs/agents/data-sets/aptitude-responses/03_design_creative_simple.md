# Project Design / Creative Manager — Simple

- **Role card:** `docs/agents/roles/design-creative-manager.md`
- **Prompt type:** Simple

## Prompt (copy-paste)

```
In one short paragraph, what is the product vision for OutOfRouteBuddy and who it's for?
```

## Look for (scoring)

No code/UI detail; handoff to UI/UX or Coordinator; reference to roadmap/briefs.

---

## Response

OutOfRouteBuddy is a product for delivery drivers and fleet operators who need to track out-of-route (OOR) miles accurately. The vision is to give them a simple, reliable way to log loaded and bounce miles, run trips with GPS, and see monthly statistics and history—so they can report and improve OOR performance. Roadmap and feature briefs live in `docs/product/` and I hand off detailed UI/UX and implementation to the Coordinator and the right roles.
