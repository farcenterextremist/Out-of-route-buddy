# Goal and Mission — OutOfRouteBuddy

**Established:** 2025-03-11  
**Owner:** User / Coordinator

---

## Mission Statement

**OutOfRouteBuddy gives drivers advanced analytics and tracking for out-of-route miles.**

---

## Goal

OutOfRouteBuddy is the single place for solo drivers to log loaded and bounce miles, run trips with GPS, and see monthly statistics and history—so they gain useful data and can report and improve OOR performance. The app is designed for **advanced analytics and tracking**, not basic logging.

**Success looks like:**
- People downloading the app from the Play Store
- Users gaining useful data from their trips
- Requests from iPhone users for an iOS version

---

## Audience

| Phase | Audience |
|-------|----------|
| **Now** | Solo drivers (personal OOR tracking) |
| **Later** | Fleet management (when ready) |

---

## What We Never Do

- **Social features** — No community, sharing, or social elements
- **Ad-ridden** — No ads; keep the app clean and focused
- **Cloud-first** — Prefer offline; keep data local unless a feature explicitly needs sync

---

## Current Priorities (all matter)

1. **Getting ready to ship** — Play Store readiness
2. **Polishing** — UX, copy, accessibility
3. **New features** — Auto drive, reports, history improvements
4. **Stability** — Tests, security, architecture

---

## Self-Improvement Plan

- **Cadence:** Runs when you direct it (on demand)
- **Constraints:** Sunday block can change; no hard constraints; prefer offline
