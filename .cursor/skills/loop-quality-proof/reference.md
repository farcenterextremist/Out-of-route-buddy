# Reference — Quality Standards

## Source links

- ISO/IEC 25010 overview: <https://iso25000.com/en/iso-25000-standards/iso-25010>
- DORA metrics guide: <https://dora.dev/guides/dora-metrics/>
- Google SRE monitoring and four golden signals: <https://sre.google/sre-book/monitoring-distributed-systems/>

## Why these were selected

- ISO/IEC 25010 gives a product-quality structure.
- DORA gives delivery/stability metrics.
- SRE golden signals give operational health framing.

Together they cover product, delivery, and operations quality.
