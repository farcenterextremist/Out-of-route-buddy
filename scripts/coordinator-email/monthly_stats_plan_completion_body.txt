End trip vs Clear trip (so you know)
- End Trip: Saves the trip and stores it. It counts toward your monthly statistics and appears in trip history and calendar. Use when the trip is real and you want it recorded.
- Clear Trip: Does not save the trip. It will not count toward monthly stats and will not appear in history or calendar. Use for bad trips, test runs, or when you want to discard.

Metadata and security (from the plan)
- Trip data stays local (Room); no PII in logs; optional later: EncryptedSharedPreferences or SQLCipher if you need stronger hardening.
- Metadata: document or extend trip metadata in a structured way (Back-end); avoid free-form blobs that could contain sensitive info.
- Clear trip is recommended for discarding bad or test data so it never persists.
